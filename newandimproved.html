Small Town to Big City

Born in 1986. Abington Memorial Hospital. After shuffling around for awhile, roots were planted in Souderton, Pennsylvania - a small place with a lot of grass, a lot of cows, and that's about it. Souderton is the place everyone comes back to after they graduate from college - safe, quiet, perfect place to raise a family. And I couldn't wait to leave.

I very quickly became an athlete, playing soccer, softball and volleyball and by the time I was in seventh grade I was on four different soccer teams. I ended up riding that wave past high school graduation onto a semi-professional team in Philadelphia, where I played for three years. I declined a fourth year to move out of cowtown into downtown Philadelphia and have lived here for the past seven years.

Philadelphia Freedom

Since saying 'See Ya Later' to my hometown in 2010, Philadelphia has brought me an entirely new life, filled with new adventures, travels, and people.

Life has been filled with Sunday's in the fall spent with friends watching football, to dancing and feasting on late night eats on Saturday night's being followed by painful workouts on Sunday mornings. I've found lately that planning for my future and spending quality time on my couch with netflix has provided some much needed balance!
